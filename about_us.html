About Indiancurryrecipez.co.uk

Indian food is different from rest of the world not only in taste but also in cooking methods. It reflects a perfect blend of various cultures and ages. Just like Indian culture, food in India has also been influenced by various civilizations, which have contributed their share in its overall development and the present form. The love for food all started in my family as I saw my father, who was a chef, make yummy tasty meals and give me so many tips about food each time I saw him cook. I was born and brought up in India and having experienced living in the most multi-cultural city - Mumbai also known as Bombay in the past, travelling to different parts of India, I have always been fascinated with foods from the different regions. I have compiled these recipes on my website - indiancurryrecipez.co.uk
    – Margaret Pinto

About the app

We believe that anybody can cook and this app will help you decide what to cook, give you easy to follow step by step instructions with video to simplify your cooking and help you learn the authentic way to cook. You will not find me talking for hours on end, just straight in the pan and let’s cook. This app will let you save your favourite recipes on the phone or share it with friends. You could pick ingredients from recipes and add to the grocery list to better manage your shopping saving you time.
